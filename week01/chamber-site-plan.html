Analina Del Vecchio - WDD 230

Site Name

Oly Chamber of Commerce

I chose this name because I live in Olympia, Washington. "Oly" is a unique and recognizable term that all the residents of the city identify with. It is a charming and friendly way to represent our beloved city, which we hold dear. Additionally, this is where the Chamber of Commerce operates.

Site Purpose

The Oly Chamber of Commerce acts as an engine of local economic development, a meeting place for business leaders where all are welcome. We work together with local businesses, entrepreneurs and organizations to foster innovation, support sustainable growth and highlight the unique strengths that unite us. Olympia is more than a city it is a vibrant community and our Chamber is here to connect, strengthen and empower all of its members.
