Demographics and Statistical Data

  • Population: Approximately 55,583 people.
  • Demographics: 51% female and 49% male.
  • Diversity: 75.9% White, 6.7% Asian, 2.6% Black, 1% Native American, and 10.3% Hispanic.
  • Median household income: $76,930 USD.
  • Poverty rate: 14.8%.
  • Median property value: $457,900 USD.
